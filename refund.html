Refund & Cancellation Policy

At Yogeo Analytics, we are committed to providing a valuable and transparent learning experience. We understand that sometimes plans change, and we have designed a fair refund and cancellation policy to support our customers.

1. Course Cancellation by Customer

If you wish to cancel your enrollment, you may request a cancellation by contacting our support team at yogeoanalytics@gmail.com within 3 days of enrollment.

  • A full refund will be issued if cancellation is requested before the course access is granted.
  • If course access has been granted but less than 10% of the content has been accessed, a 75% refund is applicable.
  • No refunds will be issued after 7 days or if more than 10% of the content has been consumed.

2. Course Cancellation by Yogeo Analytics

We reserve the right to cancel any course due to insufficient enrollments or unforeseen circumstances. In such cases, 100% of the fee will be refunded or an alternative course will be offered at no extra cost.
